Understanding the words

What they built

  • Language detection — Dzongkha vs English, by script (Unicode), instant and exact.
  • Intent classification — bilingual keyword rules across land, tax, civil registry.
  • Entity extraction — pulls out CID, plot, tax year, document type with regex.

Why it's strong

  • Fast, offline, deterministic — no API key, no model to host.
  • Exact by design — an 11-digit CID is copied character-for-character, never "rephrased".
  • Bilingual from day one.
  • Proven reusable — the product adopted this logic verbatim.

Why It Reshapes Everything
The lens
"More" is not "better" here

The constraints reward the opposite of complexity

What the goal punishes

  • Many AI calls per question — 4+ calls multiply cost & latency.
  • A brain welded to a proprietary cloud (e.g. Groq) — can't move on-prem.
  • Heavy, multi-service stacks — fragile and expensive to run.

What the goal rewards

  • One call per question — cheap, fast, easy to scale.
  • Open-weights model you can download and host yourself.
  • A light footprint + deterministic code where it's cheaper and safer.

This is the lens for every keep-or-drop decision on the next slide.
